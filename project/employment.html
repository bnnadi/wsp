Employment

Nadic is a growing, consulting engineering firm and as such, we are always looking for professional and technical talent to add to our team.Nadic is an Equal Opportunity Employment (EOE) and Drug-Free Workplace firm.

We have career opportunities for entry-level and experienced technicians, engineers in water resources, geotechnical engineering, construction inspection, environmental engineering and geology. Additional careers are available in the following areas: Laboratory Testing, Business Development, Administration and Management.

If you are a talented, open-minded engineer, technician, or administrator looking for personal growth, challenge, or for the opportunity to grow with a young, viable company, then Nadic Engineering Services, Inc. would like to hear from you. We know that hiring and retaining the best people are the keys to our long-term success. Therefore, we are committed to providing a positive work environment. We do not offer jobs only. We offer a career.

Nadic is currently looking for qualification and talented individuals for the following positions:

  • Entry level engineers
  • Senior geotechnical engineer
  • Senior technicians
