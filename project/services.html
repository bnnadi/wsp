Services

Nadic was founded with a commitment to provide superior service to our clients. Our service areas include geo-technical, water resources, geo-environmental, and construction materials testing and inspection. Nadic's objective for each service area is to do our best to meet your goals. We accomplish all projects by understanding what is required, communicating with our clients, and being responsive to our client's needs. We bring the right experience and skill to every project. The experience of our staff allows us to present innovative, superior solutions to engineering and environmental problems. The result is a smoothly done job that is on time and within budget.
